Each painting in the Bay Area series is an exercise in imagining what an iconic sight could look like in a dystopian future, where Earth has been through an environmental disaster and all the oceans & most of the atmosphere have disappeared.
The Palace of Fine Arts painting depicts a desert in place of the San Francisco bay and the Fine Arts Palace ruined building.
The Alcatraz Island features a canyon between the Alcatraz Island and the city of San Francisco, where the ocean floor used to be.
Both paintings were inspired by science-fiction literature, especially the Dune series by Frank Herbert and NASA Perseverance rover landing on Mars.
